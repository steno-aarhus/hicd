## Questionnaire data
The following information was collected through questionnaire between Nov 18 2020 and Feb 7 2021: 
* Anthropometrics
* Dental health
* Diabetes-specific problems and motivation
* Diabetic status
* Dyspnoea
* Fertility
* Sexual dysfunction
* Sleeping problems
* Symptoms and diseases
* Taste and smell
* Well-being

## Register data
The following register data are available in the HICD. 
* Danish National Patinet Register
* Danish National Health Service Registry
* Danish National Prescription Register Statistic
* Clinical Laboratory Information System (LABKA), The Central Denmark Region
* Register of Laboratory Results for Research
* Cause of Death Register
* Statistics Denmark

Most of the registers data are available more than 20 years before inclusion in the cohort. 

## How to get access to data
Fra den nuværende hjemmeside
The HICD is managed by a steering committee at SDCA consisting of members from both epidemiological and clinical diabetes research. The steering committee strongly encourage interested researchers across discipline to use this research resource. SDCA will provide applicants with detailed information on the available data and the application procedure. To get access to data please apply to the steering committee: auh.helbredimidt@rm.dk

Research on HICD-data based on questionnaires and register data require no ethical approval of projects. All data analyses are to be performed on SDCA's remote access to Statistics Denmark's server.  Clinical studies which want to recruit participants from HICD likewise apply to the steering committee: auh.helbredimidt@rm.dk

A senior researcher affiliated with SDCA will be the contact person to a potential research project and 'and responsible for the performing of analyses at Statistics Denmark's server. The application process includes project approval by the steering committee, preparation of dataset, data extraction and access to Statistics Denmark. Normally this process takes four to six months, but will be dependent on the human resources at SDCA.
